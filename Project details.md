# 🛡️ GUARDIAN BAND – SMART WOMEN'S SAFETY WEARABLE SYSTEM

---

## 1. 📌 PROJECT TITLE

📍 **Guardian Band – Smart Women's Safety Wearable System**

---

## 2. 🚨 PROBLEM STATEMENT

Women often face safety challenges while traveling alone, especially during emergencies.

Existing safety solutions may not provide immediate assistance, real-time location tracking, or hands-free activation during critical situations.

There is a need for a smart, wearable, and reliable safety system that can instantly notify trusted contacts and share live location when a user is in danger.

---
